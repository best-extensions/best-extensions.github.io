Page Customizer

Tired of websites with tiny fonts, harsh colors, or annoying layouts With Page Customizer, you can tweak the appearance of any site in real time. Its like having your own stylesheet no coding required!

Change fonts, background colors, text sizes, spacing, visibility, and more. Whether you're making a page easier to read or want to hide elements that distract you, this extension puts full control in your hands.

Perfect for accessibility, personalization, and focused reading. Your settings are saved per site, so your preferences stay even when you reload.

📄How to Use

  1. Install from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Open any webpage you want to customize.
  3. Click the Page Customizer icon in your browser toolbar.
  4. Use the controls to adjust font size, spacing, background, and more.
  5. Changes apply immediately and are remembered for each site.

✅ Features

  • Live edit font size, family, and colors
  • Hide elements like headers, footers, sidebars
  • Set background and text colors for readability
  • Save settings per site automatically
  • Lightweight and privacy-first
