Free Image Rounder

Want to turn your square or rectangular images into perfect circles Meet Free Image Rounder a super simple extension that lets you upload an image and apply a perfectly rounded mask with adjustable radius.

Whether youre designing a profile picture, preparing images for social media, or just love the rounded aesthetic this tool gets the job done in seconds. Upload your image, use the slider to set the desired roundness, and export the result. Its visual, intuitive, and doesnt require any photo editing skills.

Bonus: the image remains sharp, retains original resolution, and you dont lose quality like with online converters. Plus, it works fully offline no uploads or external servers needed.

📄How to Use

  1. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Click the extension icon to open the interface.
  3. Upload your image using the file selector.
  4. Use the slider to choose how round you want it (from 1% to 100%).
  5. Preview and then save your rounded image as PNG.

✅ Features

  • Instant image rounding with live preview
  • Adjust roundness from slight to full circle
  • High-resolution output
  • Works completely offline
  • Supports PNG, JPG, and WebP input
