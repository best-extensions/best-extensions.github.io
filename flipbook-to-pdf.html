Flipbook to PDF Downloader

📚 Tired of not being able to save digital flipbooks for offline reading? Meet Flipbook to PDF Downloader, the ultimate solution for converting online flipbooks from multiple platforms into downloadable PDF files with just one click!

This powerful Chrome extension works seamlessly across all major flipbook platforms including Archive.org, FlipSnack.com, FlipHTML5.com, Heyzine.com, AnyFlip, and Google Books. Whether you're researching, studying, or simply enjoying digital publications, this tool lets you preserve your favorite flipbooks as high-quality PDF documents.

🚀 The extension automatically scans through all pages of a flipbook, extracts high-resolution images (including tricky blob-based content), removes duplicates, and compiles everything into a polished PDF. No more manual screenshotting or dealing with incomplete captures!
