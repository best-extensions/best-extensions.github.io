Smart Volume Booster

🔊 Smart Volume Booster is the ultimate Chrome extension for enhancing your audio experience while browsing the web. Whether you're watching YouTube videos, streaming music, or attending an online meeting, this tool gives you full control over your browser's audio output — in real-time!

With just a few clicks, you can increase the volume of any tab up to 500%, apply professional-grade equalizer presets (Bass Boost, Treble, Vocal), and even activate an intelligent audio normalizer to ensure consistent sound levels across videos. This extension is ideal for users with low speaker output, quiet content, or those who just love to fine-tune their sound. 🎧

And that’s not all — the PRO version unlocks powerful extra features like a multi-band EQ 🎚️, pitch shift (coming soon), stereo panning, and reverb effects for immersive surround-style audio. All of this is delivered in a clean, user-friendly interface that doesn’t require any technical skills. Just drag, slide, and enjoy!

🚀 How to Use

  1. Install Smart Volume Booster from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Open any tab with audio or video content (e.g., YouTube, Spotify, Netflix).
  3. Click the extension icon in your browser.
  4. Adjust the volume slider, pick an EQ preset, or toggle effects like normalization.
  5. Click "Save" to keep your settings per domain.
