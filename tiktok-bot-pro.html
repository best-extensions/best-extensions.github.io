TikTok Bot Pro

Tired of spending hours on manual engagement? 🚀 TikTok Bot Pro is the ultimate automation tool that helps you grow your TikTok presence without the endless scrolling and clicking. This powerful Chrome extension automates likes, comments, follows, and even extracts user lists—all with just a few clicks.

Whether you're a content creator looking to expand your audience, a marketer managing multiple accounts, or simply someone who wants to grow their following efficiently, TikTok Bot Pro gives you the tools you need. The extension works directly within TikTok's interface, providing a seamless experience with a beautiful dark-themed overlay that lets you monitor progress in real-time.

One of the standout features is the User Extractor 📥. You can extract followers, following lists, users from hashtags, or even commenters from any post. Export them to a TXT file or use them directly for your automation campaigns. No more manual copying—everything is automated and organized.

The Auto Like and Auto Comment features let you engage with content automatically. Set your target (hashtag or user list), configure delays between actions to stay safe, and let the bot do the work. Import your own comment list from a TXT file and the extension will rotate through them naturally. You can even enable auto-like while commenting for maximum engagement!

Need to follow or unfollow users in bulk? The Bulk Follow/Unfollow feature handles it effortlessly. Load your user list, set the delay, and watch as the extension processes each profile automatically. Smart delays between actions help mimic human behavior and keep your account safe.

🔧 How to Use

  1. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store
  2. Open TikTok in your browser (the extension will open it automatically if not open)
  3. Click the extension icon to open the control panel
  4. Choose your task: Extract, Like, Comment, or Follow
  5. Configure your settings (target, delays, user list)
  6. Click Start and monitor progress in the floating popup
  7. Stop anytime with the Stop button

✅ Features

  • User Extractor: Extract followers, following, hashtag users, and commenters
  • Auto Like: Automatically like posts by hashtag or from user list
  • Auto Comment: Post comments with rotating message list
  • Bulk Follow/Unfollow: Mass follow or unfollow from your user list

💡 Pro Tips

  • Use realistic delays (5-15 seconds) to mimic human behavior
  • Don't run 24/7—take breaks between sessions
  • Start with small numbers and increase gradually
  • Mix automated and manual engagement for best results
  • Use quality, relevant comments—not spam
