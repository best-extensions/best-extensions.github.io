URL & Email Extractor

URL & Email Extractor is a smart and efficient Chrome extension that helps you extract email addresses from a list of URLs. It automates the process of visiting each URL, scanning the webpage, and collecting any email addresses it can find saving you hours of manual effort.

Just paste in your list of websites, click "Start Extraction", and watch the results roll in. Each found email is neatly listed next to the URL it came from. It's perfect for digital marketers, outreach specialists, recruiters, and researchers who need clean, organized contact lists fast.

You can choose to ignore generic or no-reply addresses, and even export the results to a file. It's fast, lightweight, and completely privacy-friendly no data ever leaves your browser.

📄How to Use

  1. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Click the extension icon and paste in a list of URLs.
  3. Choose any filters (e.g. ignore "noreply" addresses).
  4. Click "Start" to begin scanning websites for emails.
  5. Review the results, copy them, or export as CSV.

✅ Features

  • Extracts emails from a list of URLs, automatically
  • Skips duplicates and generic patterns if desired
  • Optional delay between scans to ensure content loads
  • Download results as CSV or copy to clipboard
  • Works entirely offline in your browser
