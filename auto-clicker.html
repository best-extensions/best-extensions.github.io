Common questions

FAQ

Does it work on HTML5 games and Canvas elements?
Yes! Auto Clicker Pro dispatches the full event chain including PointerEvents (pointermove, pointerdown, pointerup) and MouseEvents. This makes it compatible with modern HTML5 game engines, Canvas-based apps, and WebGL applications that other auto clickers can't handle.
Why does the popup close when I press Start?
This is by design. When the popup is open, it captures browser focus, which prevents clicks from reaching the target page (especially in games). Closing the popup ensures clicks are dispatched directly to the page. A live overlay appears showing real-time progress.
How do I stop the auto clicker?
Press Ctrl+M (or open the popup and click Stop). A "Auto Clicker Stopped" notification will briefly appear on the page to confirm. The auto clicker also stops automatically when the configured number of clicks is reached.
What's the difference between Free and Pro?
The free version includes all core features but limits the minimum interval to 0.5 seconds, maximum clicks to 100, and multi-points to 3. The Pro version removes all limits — intervals as fast as 0.01s, unlimited clicks, and unlimited multi-point positions.
Does it collect any data?
No. Auto Clicker Pro runs entirely in your browser. It makes zero network requests, collects no analytics, and stores all settings locally using Chrome's storage API. Your privacy is fully respected.
