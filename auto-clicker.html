Auto Clicker

Automate repetitive clicking tasks on your screen with ease! Auto Clicker lets you record a position and repeat clicks at custom intervals great for games, testing, or automation.

Just click Record, select a position, and let it do the rest. This extension is lightweight, fast, and doesnt require any permissions outside the page.

📄How to Use

  1. Install from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Click the extension icon and press Record.
  3. Click on the screen where you want to automate the click.
  4. Set the interval and press "Start" to begin auto-clicking.
  5. Press Q to stop the process.

✅ Features

  • Click any point on the screen at intervals
  • Simple recording and trigger system
  • Fast and lightweight
  • No extra permissions needed
  • Stop anytime with Q
