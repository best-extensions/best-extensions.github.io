My Notes and Reminders

Ever wish your browser had a built-in notepad Thats where My Notes and Reminders comes in! Its like sticking post-it notes inside Chrome but digital, searchable, and organized. Whether youre jotting down a grocery list, tracking tasks, or saving random ideas, this extension has your back.

This extension is lightweight, fast, and super easy to use. There's no complex setup just click the icon, type your note, and hit save. You can create multiple notes, color-code them, pin important ones, and even set reminders so Chrome will nudge you later. Great for students, professionals, and forgetful geniuses alike.

📄How to Use

  1. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Click the notepad icon in the toolbar.
  3. Type your note, to-do list, or reminder.
  4. Click Save and boom! Youre done.
  5. Need a nudge Set a reminder and Chrome will notify you!
