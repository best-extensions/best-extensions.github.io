PDFinFLIP - PDF to Flipbook

Convert your static PDFs into stunning interactive flipbooks with PDFinFLIP! Its perfect for eBooks, magazines, reports, catalogs and more.

Just upload a PDF, and in seconds, youll get a page-flipping animation with sound, double-page view, and beautiful navigation all inside your browser. No coding. No setup. Just flip!

📄How to Use

  1. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Open the extension and upload your PDF file.
  3. Wait a few seconds for conversion.
  4. View your PDF with realistic page-flip effect.
  5. Click Open in New Window to view fullscreen or save it as HTML.

✅ Features

  • Flipbook animation with sound
  • Supports double-page view
  • Zoom and fullscreen support
  • Exportable as HTML file
  • Custom background options
  • Bookmark icon for each page
